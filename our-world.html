Coming from the civic sector, I know the financial struggles many organizations face.

Since most civic organizations are funded through projects with strictly defined and limiting budgetary lines, their budgets often don't account for consultants such as myself.

That's why 10% of what you spend with me goes directly into a fund that provides NGOs with the same services you will have received.

By choosing to work with me, you're not just investing in your future - you're supporting the growth of organizations that need it most. Your support helps extend the transformative benefits of coaching and mentorship to NGOs, enabling them to create a positive impact in their communities and achieve their missions.

Together, we can make a real difference, one project at a time. If you'd like to know more about the NGOs I work with, let's talk about it during our first session.
